Welcome to "History of Physics: The Game!" (name and game in progress). In this game you manage the research done by a nondescript civilization from the dawn of humankind to the near future.
This is still very much a work in progress, and many parts we intended to implement have yet to work. For example, the "science" resource does not do anything yet, but it will eventually impact the cost of new technologies and increase the chances of good events happening.
You must pay for new technologies with the gold that you have, but there is no limit on how many technologies you may research, or anything stopping you from spending more gold than you have (although this will be fixed).
To begin the game, click on one or more of the cards below, and then click the "end turn" button. Your objective is to get as much happiness as possible for your civilization.
